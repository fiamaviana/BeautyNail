Professional Manicure

Acrylic

It’s a combination of a liquid and powder that creates a hard protective layer over your natural nail and false tips. The mixture hardens from exposure to air and creates a transparent canvas for regular nail polish.

How long it takes: about 1.5 hours for a full set and 45 minutes for a fill.

How long it lasts: Two to three weeks.

Cost: €40 for a full set, €15 for a fill.

Paraffin Manicure

A parrafin manicure involves dipping your hand in paraffin wax—a colorless, odorless wax derived from beeswax and petroleum—and placing them in plastic gloves and wrapping in a hot towel.

How long it takes: about 30 min to an hour.

How it affects nail health: It won't affect your nails as it's a natural ingredient'.

Special title treatment

Instead of a lacquer, the color comes from a pigmented powder. Between base coats and a sealant, you dip your nails into a little jar of your chosen color. The powder will only stick to the sealant, so it leaves less room for messy cuticles.

How long it takes: about 45 min.

How long it lasts: Up to three to four weeks without chips.

Cost: €35 per application.

Gel

Also known as “hard gel,” it can be applied over natural or fake nails. The main difference from Shellac (or soak-off gel) is that you can extend the length of the nail, which can be sculpted and shaped just like acrylic.

How long it takes: about 45 min to an hour.

How long it lasts: two or three weeks.

Cost: €40 per application.

Shellac

It’s basically a hybrid of gel and nail polish that’s cured and hardened with a UV light. The word Shellac is actually just the trademark name of CND, the first company to invent and register this type of polish.

How long it takes: about 45 min to an hour.

How long it lasts: about two or three weeks.

Cost: €35 per application.
